Concept: Rational numbers on the number line

A rational number is any number that can be written in the form p/q, where p and q are integers and q ≠ 0. We can show rational numbers on a number line like points.

Teach Step 1 / 9 – Rational numbers and fractions

In this sample Teach step, we treat rational numbers as fractions p/q and practise identifying numerator, denominator and sign.

Board – Explanation & examples
  • Any fraction like 3/5 is a rational number.
  • −2/7 is also a rational number (negative sign in numerator).
  • 0 can be written as 0/1, 0/5, etc. (q ≠ 0).
